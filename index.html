I'm a current senior at Harvard College studying computer science, with a concurrent masters in computational science and engineering.

I was born and raised in Bacolod City, went to boarding school in the foothills of the Himalayas in India, and now call the perplexing one-way streets of Boston my home.

I love meeting new people and learning about their stories.
